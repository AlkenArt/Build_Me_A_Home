Home Pooling/ Joint Ventures

We bring in a unique concept called home pooling where 2 or more people(friends/colleagues/acquaintances/like minded people) can invest together in one single project(for self-use/Commercial/Resale). By doing this they will able to save at least 40% of their cost of constructing structure. This is a win-win situation

Video Intro

Elaboration

If 3 people invest and buy a 2400sq/ft of land(registered in all their names) and construct a 3 floored spacious 4BHK with each floor customized as per owners requirement, technically they own 800sqft of land i.e 33.33% however they are able to live in a spacious 2400sqft. The advantage of this is that the owners not just own the flat but also the land.

By doing this a person will be able to own a home/flat worth 1.5 crores in just 80-85 Lakhs, this is close to 50% of savings
